About us

At CashOut, our motto is "Time is Money," so it's safe to say that we highly value our personal time. We have on record that the average American spends around two years of their life waiting in line. To us folk at CashOut, that's nothing short of a real-life nightmare, so we decided to do something about it.

To help combat these unnerving queues, we are happy to introduce our newest technology: CashOut. With CashOut, customers spend a little less time waiting around, and a little more time doing what matters most.

How It Works

While the technology is far from simple, the implementation is just that. Using RFID chips and our software, customers can pay for their items in no time at all. They simply open the free-to-download mobile application, scan the RFID tag on their item, and pay for it right then and there via their mobile device. The money is directly transferred from their funds to the retail location.

At CashOut, we care deeply about our clients' security. As a result of an implementation of pre-existing RFID functionality, users of CashOut may not leave a store without first paying for their items. Once payment is confirmed, the security feature of the RFID tag is deactivated and the customer may safely leave the store. Until then, the security system is fully in effect.

Our Mobile App

Our mobile application is for Android devices and acts as an interface between the user and the RFID technology. With the application a customer may scan an item, add it to their virtual shopping cart, and check out when they are ready to leave.
